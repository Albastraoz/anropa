ABOUT US

Intro

We are a crewing agency based in Ub, Serbia. Our aim is to provide a chance for serbian people to find work abroad. And for employers to find the so much needed skilled workers in europe and beyond.

We cover many sectors like seasonal work, skilled labour (electricians, plummers, carpenters, welders etc.), Maritime (officers and crew), gastromeny, housekeeping etc.
